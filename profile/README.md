## 👋 Welcome to Aas-Paas

Aas-Paas is a **hyperlocal commerce infrastructure** platform that helps local businesses launch branded websites and apps with integrated last-mile delivery.

This organization hosts the ecosystem of templates, apps, and infrastructure that power the Aaspaas local delivery platform for restaurants, hospitals, retail stores, and multi-vendor complexes.

***

## 🌏 What Aas-Paas Does

- Builds the **storefront layer** (websites and apps) for local businesses using template-based projects like `aaspaas`.
- Operates the **logistics layer**, combining full-time riders and gig delivery partners into one hybrid network.
- Targets underserved local businesses that need modern digital ordering, tracking, and operations without building everything from scratch.

***

## 🏥🍽 Target Verticals

The ecosystem focuses on a few core verticals where local presence and fast delivery matter most.

- Restaurants & food dining – menus, ordering, and live delivery tracking.
- Hospitals & healthcare – appointments, pharmacy orders, and OPD/queue workflows.
- Retail stores – product catalogs, inventory, and local on-demand delivery.
- Malls & multi-vendor complexes – multi-store dashboards and unified ordering.

***

## 🚴 Delivery Network Vision

Aas-Paas aims to standardize hyperlocal delivery as a shared infrastructure rather than every business reinventing it.
- Full-time riders for premium or high-volume clients needing reliability and branding.
- Open gig network where anyone with a bike can sign up, deliver, and get paid via UPI.
- Built-in KYC, ratings, and basic compliance to keep the network safe and trustworthy.
